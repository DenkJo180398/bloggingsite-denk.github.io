Economic growth

Posted by Denk Jonas

11th October 2021

One simple thing in economy is, two similar companies can´t grow both at the same time. As a result of this, if one company grows another similar one must decrease.

A simple example: If the company Adidas let their shoes produce by a different company in another country, the new commissioned company would grow brutally while the old producing brand would go to insolvency.
